osu!

osu! is a rhythm game created by Dean "peppy" Herbert.

The primary gameplay of osu! is control the cursor to follow the rhythm such as clicking the circle, sliding the bar and rotating the spinner with a mouse, tablet or touch screen.

There are also different mode of osu! including osu!mania, osu!taiko and osu!catch that provide different method of gameplay of you to discover.

In osu!, all the different song is called "beatmap", which created by different beatmap artist where you can download in the official webpage without worry about suspicious file problem.

There are also scoring and ranking system in every of the beatmap for players to compete.
