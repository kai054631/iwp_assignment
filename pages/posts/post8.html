Grand Theft Auto V

price=US$26.70
Platform=Xbox,PC,PlayStation
Publisher(s):Rockstar Games
Genre(s)=Action-adventure


Gameplay

Grand Theft Auto V is an action-adventure game played from either a third-person or first-person perspective. Players complete missions linear scenarios with set objectives to progress through the story.There will be alot of mission to complete in order to play new mission.Other than playing mission, players can freely roam the open world.

Players use weapon to attack enemy.Players control the three lead protagonists throughout single-player and switch among them, both during and outside missions. They is 3 main character which are Michael De Santa (retired bank robber), Franklin Clinton(street gangster), and Trevor Philips (drug dealer and gunrunner). They will attempt to commit heists while under pressure from a corrupted government agency and powerful criminals.
