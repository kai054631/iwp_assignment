Gameplay

Among Us is a online multiplayer game (four to fifteen players) developed and published by American game studio Innersloth. Up to three players are randomly and secretly chosen to be the Impostor each round. The Imposters has 1 or 2 ways to win the game by killing most of the crew or by sabotaging a critical system on the map. The crewmates can win by completing all the task or by identifying and ejecting all the imposters.

If a Crewmate is killed, they become a ghost. They can pass through walls, spectate other players game and can chat with other ghost. At start of the game, each crewmember are assign a task to complete such as fixing wires, downloading data and more.Any living players can have a group call by reporting dead body or by pressing the emergency meeting button.
